About Cleartrip

Launched in July 2006, Cleartrip Pvt Ltd, a Flipkart company, has emerged as India's fastest-growing online travel technology company. In April 2021, Flipkart acquired a majority stake in Cleartrip. Cleartrip recently emerged as the no. 2 OTA player as per a study by VIDEC. With an aggressive plan to emerge as a leading innovator in the industry, Cleartrip is on its way to building a differentiated value proposition for its customers looking for end-to-end travel solutions. With industry-first offerings including ClearChoice Plus, ClearChoice Max and Cancel for No Reason!, Cleartrip has a clear vision to provide innovative solutions in the OTA segment. Combining intuitive products with a customer-centric approach and a wide selection of flights, hotels, buses and holiday packages, Cleartrip brings a unique selling point to the market, offering its customers convenience, choice, competitive prices, and premium content.

How Does Cleartrip Help You Plan Your Travel?

Cleartrip offers a collection of everything you need for a perfect trip. All can be done simultaneously, from flights to taxis to hotel bookings. This is how planning flights and hotels with Cleartrip gives you a clear edge:-

ClearChoice Max: It offers free cancellation or rescheduling for domestic (up to 24 hours before departure) and international flights (up to 72 hours before departure).

ClearChoice Plus: Free date or airline change up to 12 hours (or 24 hours for Air India* and Vistara*) before departure.

Easy hotel cancellation: You can quickly cancel your hotel stay. There are no costs for hotel cancellations up to 24 hours before check-in at over 20,000 hotels.

Instant refund initiation: All flight and hotel cancellation refunds are processed immediately.

Medi-cancel refund: Cancel your domestic flight booking for sound medical reasons and receive up to INR 3500 against airline cancellation charges per person per segment.

International travel insurance: For only INR 89 per user daily, you can get stress-free coverage against a wide range of uncertainties for all foreign destinations.

Special fares for armed personnel, senior citizens, and student travellers: Cleartrip has introduced lower pricing on domestic flights for armed personnel, senior citizens, and students. Coupon codes provide further discounts.

How to Book Flights on Cleartrip?

Booking flights on Cleartrip is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to secure your domestic or international flight tickets:

  • Enter Your Travel Details - Select your departure and arrival locations, travel dates, and the number of passengers.
  • Compare Flight Options - Browse through available airlines, fares, and schedules.
  • Select the Best Flight - Choose a flight that fits your budget and timing.
  • Complete the Booking - Enter passenger details, apply any available offers, and make a secure payment.
  • Receive Instant Confirmation - Get an e-ticket and travel details in your inbox.

Whether you need to book domestic flights for a weekend getaway or book international flights for a vacation abroad, Cleartrip provides a smooth and convenient experience.
